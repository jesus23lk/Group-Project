How to Play

The goal is to clear the grid without clicking on a mine. Use the following controls:

  • Left-click: Reveal a cell.
  • Right-click: Mark a cell as a mine.

Difficulty Levels

You can choose between three levels:

  • Easy: Small grid with 10 mines.
  • Medium: Medium grid with 40 mines.
  • Hard: Large grid with 99 mines.
